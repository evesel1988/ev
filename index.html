About Me

My name is Evgenia. I'm a creative designer from Moscow, Russia who at the moment lives in Amsterdam. I have a broad design expertise and enjoy working in multidisciplinary projects. I got a MSc in New Media Design from Tilburg University where I conducted research on visualizing cancer treatment outcomes. Now I work as designer in Amsterdam-based entertainment company. Besides working with UX/UI design, I am well experienced in front-end and web development. I'm also involved in production of visual, animated and video content.
